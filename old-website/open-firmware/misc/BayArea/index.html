About This Page

This page contains hotel and travel information for San Francisco Bay Area Open Firmware Working Group meetings.

Firmworks, Mountain View, CA

Firmworks has maps, directions and links to local community information.

Hotels Near Firmworks

  • Hyatt Rickeys, Palo Alto.

    Hyatt Rickeys is a nice hotel about 5 minutes from Firmworks.

  • Holiday Inn, Palo Alto.

    The Holiday Inn, Palo Alto, is about 10 - 15 minutes from Firmworks and is located right off University Ave, in the the center of downtown Palo Alto, near the Palo Alto CalTrain station. Walk to University Ave and Stanford University Campus. This is a rather nice Holiday Inn.

Apple, Cuppertino, CA

The Apple R&D Campus is located near I-280 and De Anza Blvd, in Cuppertino, CA. Directions to the Apple campus are available.

Hotels Near Apple.

  • Cuppertino Inn, Cuppertino, CA.

    Near I-280 across from the Apple R&D campus. Apple has a corporate rate for visitors. Phone: 408-996-7700.
