Title:          CHRP Device bindings, Version 1.0

Author:         John Kingman

Date:           May 29, 1996

Ed/Tech:        Editorial

Synopsis:       CHRP Device bindings, Version 1.0 are available for OFWG
                Review.

Doc & Version:  PowerPC Microprocessor Common Hardware Resource Platform
                (CHRP) Device bindings, Version 1.0

Problem:        First Draft Documents Available

Proposal:

The first version of the each of the CHRP Device bindings are available on 
the Open Firwmare repository playground.sun.com.  The following are the
subject bindings:

  CHRP ISA Audio Device Binding, Unapproved Draft version 1.0, May 6, 1996
  CHRP ISA Floppy Controller Device Binding, Unapproved Draft version 1.0, 
    May 6, 1996
  CHRP ISA Keyboard/Mouse Controller Device Binding, Unapproved Draft 
    version 1.0, May 6, 1996
  CHRP ISA DMA Controller Device Binding, Unapproved Draft version 1.0, 
    May 21, 1996
  CHRP ISA Interrupt Controller Device Binding, Unapproved Draft version 
    1.0, May 21, 1996
  CHRP Linear Frame Buffer Device Binding, Unapproved Draft version 1.0, 
    May 6, 1996
  CHRP ISA Parallel Port Device Binding, Unapproved Draft version 1.0, 
    May 21, 1996
  CHRP ISA Serial Port Device Binding, Unapproved Draft version 1.0, 
    May 6, 1996
  CHRP VGA Device Binding, Unapproved Draft version 1.0, May 6, 1996

The documents are available in both postscript and HTML at

  http://playground.sun.com/1275/home.html

    or via ftp at:

  ftp://playground.sun.com/pub/1275/bindings/devices/

John Kingman
