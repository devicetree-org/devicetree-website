Directions to MCD

From the Phoenix airport:

  1. Take 143 South, Hohokam Expressway (There are also signs saying to Highway 10).
  2. Hohokam Expressway turns into 48th street. First light is Broadway continue south on 48th.
  3. The second light is Roeser/Alameda turn left, east, on Alameda.
  4. Alameda curves South, and MCG is on the corner.
  5. Turn in the first or second entrance.
  6. There is visitor parking in front.
  7. Sign in at the Gaurd desk and they will call me to come and escort you in.
