Meeting Schedule.

We meet approximately every 6 weeks or so and have been alternating our meetings between the San Francisco Bay Area and the Austin, Texas area.

We generally try to schedule the next 3 meetings in advance, to allow everybody to plan their schedules and travel.

Download or view our future meeting schedule.
